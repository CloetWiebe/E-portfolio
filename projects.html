Mediscan app

The next project was a collaboration with Delaware, a company that approached us with a case study involving the "four-eye principle," a safety measure used in hospitals to ensure accurate medication administration. This principle requires two nurses: one to administer the medication to the patient and the other to verify the dosage.

Delaware tasked us with finding a solution to improve this process. In response, we developed a progressive web app that utilizes Optical Character Recognition (OCR) technology and a custom-trained AI model. The AI is capable of recognizing medication packaging, reading the dosage, and identifying the type of medication. The system then cross-references this information with an API to ensure accuracy.

This project provided us with a great opportunity to develop and enhance several key skills:

  • Developing and deploying Progressive Web Apps (PWA)
  • Implementing Optical Character Recognition (OCR) technology
  • Training and integrating custom AI models
  • API development and data cross-referencing
  • Problem-solving in real-world healthcare scenarios

Temi Robot

My most recent project took place during my internship at HITLAB Howest, where we were tasked with creating an immersive experience for the public. The goal was to develop an AR application that would allow people to see a completed building at a life-size scale, enabling them to visualize how it would look once finished.

This application was built for the HoloLens and used QR codes as anchors to position and orient the building in the real world. The most challenging aspect of the project was ensuring that the orientation and scale of the building were accurate based on the QR codes. This required extensive work with matrices and vector calculations to achieve the correct alignment.

This project provided me with a great opportunity to develop and enhance several key skills:

  • Augmented Reality (AR) development using Microsoft HoloLens
  • QR code integration for spatial anchoring
  • Mathematical problem-solving using matrices and vector calculations
  • 3D modeling and scaling for life-size visualizations
  • Collaboration in an interdisciplinary team
